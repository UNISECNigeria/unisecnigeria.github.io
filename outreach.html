Space Outreach & Capacity-Building Project

Inspire and train the next generation of space engineers and scientists in Nigeria.

Goal

Inspire and train the next generation of space engineers and scientists in Nigeria.

Outcome

A growing national pipeline of students aware of and skilled in space technology.

Activities
  • Host workshops, webinars, and hands-on training.
  • Organize space challenges, hackathons, and summer camps.
  • Partner with schools and local STEM clubs.
